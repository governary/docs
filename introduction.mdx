Governix is the open-source governance layer for multi-tenant AWS Bedrock applications. If you're already using Bedrock for AI or RAG and now need to control which tenants can access which knowledge bases and models, track usage and cost per tenant, or produce audit evidence at the request level, Governix adds that capability as a control plane alongside your existing code — no proxy gateway required.

## What Governix is and isn't

<Note>
  Governix is a **tenant-aware AI control plane** — a governance and audit layer for Bedrock RAG. It is not a chatbot frontend, a vector database, a general-purpose orchestration suite, or a full billing platform.
</Note>

Governix handles the operational concerns that Bedrock itself doesn't address: who is allowed to call what, how much have they used, and what exactly happened. Your application code, your Bedrock setup, and your data layer stay as they are.

<Tip>
  Governix works in SDK and middleware integration modes. You do not need to route all traffic through a proxy to get policy enforcement and event recording.
</Tip>

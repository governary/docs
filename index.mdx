Governix adds the governance layer your Bedrock-based AI application needs in production. Once you're serving multiple tenants, you need to control who can use which knowledge bases and models, track usage and cost per tenant, and maintain an audit-ready record of every request — without rearchitecting your existing Bedrock integration.

## What Governix does

Governix solves three production governance problems that AWS Bedrock alone doesn't address:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enforce runtime policy">
    Before each Bedrock call, Governix evaluates tenant-specific policy — allowing, denying, filtering, or quota-blocking the request based on rules you define.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track per-tenant usage">
    After each request, Governix records token usage, request count, estimated cost, and quota consumption — broken down by tenant, application, and model.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Maintain an audit ledger">
    Every retrieval and generation event is recorded with request-level detail so you can investigate failures, explain decisions, and export evidence for compliance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Governix works as a control plane alongside your existing Bedrock integration — not as an inline proxy gateway. You keep your current setup and add governance incrementally.
</Note>
